Particle System Controls:
1, 2, 3, and 4 keys: select solver (Euler, Midpoint, Implicit, and Velocity Verlet)
R key: reset. Space-bar: single-step. P key: pause/play particles.

Cloth Controls:
Arrow Keys: move corner. F key: switch between springs and rigid constraints.

Planet Controls:
Z key: planetary gravity off/on. X key: spining force off/on. C key: world gravity on/off.

Camera Controls:
Mouse Drag: pivot camera. WASD keys: move camera. Q and E keys: raise and lower camera. Left Shift: hold while moving to increase camera speed.
